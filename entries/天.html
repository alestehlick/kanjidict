天
heavenly phenomena
Kun: ama, ame  |  On: ten
heaven ・ sky ・ uppermost

Explanation

Early graphs show the figure of a person (大) with an added stroke above to mark what is highest; thus ‘the sky/heaven.’ By extension, the supreme or celestial in ritual language.

Additional Notes

Frequently contrasts with 地 ‘earth.’ In archaic forms the top line emphasizes ‘above.’

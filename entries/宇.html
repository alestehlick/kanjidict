宇
heavenly phenomena
Kun:  |  On: u
eaves ・ roofing ・ expanse (of the heavens)

Explanation

Under a roof radical (宀) with phonetic 于; first meant the house’s covering/eaves. By semantic elevation it came to denote the overarching ‘vault/expanse,’ especially paired with 宙 in 宇宙 ‘the universe.’

Additional Notes

Architectural image → cosmic covering; the roof’s outer line is central to the metaphor.

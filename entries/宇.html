宇
heavenly phenomena
Kun:  |  On: u
eaves of a house ・ the heavens ・ universe

Explanation

Shuowen says: ‘The eaves of a house.’ It is composed of 宀 (roof) and 于. 宇 designates the eaves of a house. Because eaves spread out broadly and widely, this sense was extended and its sound was borrowed. Thus the sky, the covering vault of heaven, is called 宇.

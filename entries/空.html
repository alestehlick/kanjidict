空
heavenly phenomena
Kun: sora, kara, aku, akeru, munashii  |  On: kuu
sky ・ empty ・ void ・ vacant

Explanation

Composed of the ‘cavern/roofed hollow’ determinative (穴) and phonetic 工. Original sense: a vaulted, hollow space; later generalized to the open sky and to ‘emptiness/void.’

Additional Notes

Japanese usage splits ‘sora’ (sky) and ‘kara’ (empty). In classical descriptions may denote an arched emptiness like a dome.
